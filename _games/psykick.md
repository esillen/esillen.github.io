# Psykick
## An android game about a cute hedgehog without spikes
### This game was made by me and Jesper Norberg duing a week-long game making marathon.

### Psykick is like action mini golf with cool physics and enemies (and a super cute character). You control the hedgehog by pressing the screen and the hedgehog is "psykicked" in that direction. The goal is always to reach the end of the level. Sometimes you have unlimitied psykicks, sometimes you have a limited amount. The ultimate goal is to reach the end of the game and reunite with your children.
