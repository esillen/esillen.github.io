Gameplay footage: the two players try to get enemies in the beam between them.

### This is a collaborative game for two players. Each player controls one character and there is a laser between the two characters that grows stronger the closer they are. Kill as many enemies as you can by hitting them by the laser!
### It is also possible to play solo, even though that is not the intended way to play.
