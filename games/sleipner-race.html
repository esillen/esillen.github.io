Sleipner Race

Sleipner Race is an arcade gamed that you play together with others on a custom made big controller. The goal of the game is to reach the end of the level without falling off or having Odin (the rider and the boss of the norse gods) hit his head.

An early version of the game can be found at Simmer.io

The Game is made in Unity3D. The rune stone and the controller are wooden and the game is running on a Windows machine.
